Steak with low carb fries

A steak with low carb Keto fries

Steak with low carb fries instead of regular potato fries

Second paragraph talking about this healthy meal.

Ingredients

  • 3lbs of steak
  • 5lbs Horse raddish
  • Paprika powder

Steps

  1. Sear the steak on both ends
  2. Take stake out and wrap in tinfoil
  3. cut the fries and season them
  4. Bake in the over for 20 minutes
